# Finance base

A base with separate access for keeping sensitive financial data. In addition to all the datasets automatically synced from [[pzn-apps/en/talent-content-mgmt/Talent and Content Management System#Catalog base\|Catalog base]], contains such  [[pzn-apps/en/talent-content-mgmt/Airtable linked tables\|linked datasets]] as **Accounts**, **Transaction**, **Royalty Reports** along auxiliary tables: **Categories**, **Income/Expenses**
